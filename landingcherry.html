Undergraduate (B.Tech) Admissions

Entrance Exam: Must qualify TS EAPCET or JEE Main

Seat Allocation:

  • Category A (70% seats): Through TS EAPCET counselling → choose KITSW
  • Category B (30% seats): Management quota via institute-level procedure

Eligibility: 10+2 with Physics, Mathematics, Chemistry/Bio/Biotech

Total Seats: Approx. 1,440-1,452 overall in various branches

Fees:

  • 1.25 Lakh per year, totaling around 5 Lakh for the full 4-year course

About KITSW

Welcome to Our Prestigious Institution

Established in 1980, KITSW has grown to become one of the premier engineering institutes in Telangana. We offer state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and a vibrant campus life that fosters both academic and personal growth.

Our mission is to provide quality technical education and promote research, innovation, and entrepreneurship among students to meet global challenges.
